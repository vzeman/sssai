## Advanced Reconnaissance & OSINT

### Subdomain Enumeration
- **amass**: `amass enum -d {domain} -passive -o /output/amass-passive.txt`
- **subfinder**: `subfinder -d {domain} -o /output/subdomains.txt`
- **theHarvester**: `theHarvester -d {domain} -b all --file /output/harvester`

### Live Host Probing
- `cat /output/subdomains.txt | httpx -status-code -title -tech-detect -json -o /output/httpx.json`

### Email Security
- **checkdmarc**: `checkdmarc {domain} -f json -o /output/checkdmarc.json`
- SPF: `dig {domain} TXT +short`
- DMARC: `dig _dmarc.{domain} TXT +short`

### Subdomain Takeover
- **subjack**: `subjack -w /output/subdomains.txt -t 100 -timeout 30 -o /output/subjack.txt -ssl`
- **dnsreaper**: `dnsreaper scan --domain {domain} --out /output/dnsreaper.json --out-format json`

### Phishing & Brand Protection
- **dnstwist**: `dnstwist --registered --format json {domain} > /output/dnstwist.json`

### Certificate Transparency
- Check CT logs for additional domains/subdomains

### DNS Analysis
- **dnsrecon**: `dnsrecon -d {domain} -t std -j /output/dnsrecon.json`
- Zone transfer attempts
- DNSSEC validation: `dig +dnssec {domain}`
- **whois**: `whois {domain}`

### Technology Detection
- **whatweb**: `whatweb {target} --log-json=/output/whatweb.json`
- **wafw00f**: `wafw00f {target}` (WAF detection)

### Port Scanning
- **nmap**: `nmap -sV -sC -oN /output/nmap.txt {target}`
- **masscan**: `masscan {target} -p0-65535 --rate 1000 -oJ /output/masscan.json`
